## Our 4-Step Process

1. **Brief** (30 minutes): Call or meeting to understand objectives, target audience, and competitors. We gather everything needed to start.
2. **Design** (2-3 days): High-fidelity mockups based on your brand. Client approval before development begins.
3. **Development** (4-7 days): Coding, testing, performance and SEO optimization. Continuous status updates.
4. **Launch** (1 day): Deploy, complete training, and credential handover. You are online and independent.

Total time: 7-14 business days from project confirmation.
